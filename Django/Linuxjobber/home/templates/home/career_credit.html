Who are we?

This is who we are and what we do

We are industry professionals working to bridge the gap between the programs offered by academic institutions and the needs of the local companies. Our work builds on the existing curriculum of schools to help ensure that graduating students possess the skills that local employers are currently looking for by providing a simulation of internship to students prior to gradutation.

What happens at Career Credit?

Intrested? This are the steps involved.

Apply Now

Apply to Program

Click the Apply Now button, fill out the application and wait for approval from Careercredit and your school.

Begin Your Project

If both your school and careercredit approve your application, you'll receive work pertinent to your field of study.

Earn College Credits

If you successfully complete the work before the end of the semester, your school will grant you 3 credits.

Earn Your Certificate

You will also receive a certificate of CareerWork completion from CareerCredit at the end of the semester.
