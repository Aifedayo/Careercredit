Our Policy

Cancellation and Refund Policy

Training course registration is not complete until billing information is received in full by Linuxjobber.

Students are responsible for acquiring all materials required for learning the course in which they are enrolled.

In order to cancel or reschedule a confirmed training please submit an email request to your instructor.

Please submit all cancellation request before the first day of classes in order to receive a refund of paid registration fees. No refunds or reschedule after the first day of classes.

 
 
Cancellation by Linuxjobber

If a training class is cancelled due to any unforeseen circumstances such as weather or natural disaster, the customer is entitled to reschedule for a future training course.

Linuxjobber reserves the right to cancel any training course due to insufficient enrollment 7 days before the first day of classes. Notice will be provided with the option to reschedule for a future course date or to receive a full refund of registration fees.

Linuxjobber is not responsible for any expenses incurred by the customer if a training course is cancelled.
