How it works

What is Work Experience?

This 3 month program is designed for people who have finished technical training or school and preparing for jobs.

How Does it Work?

The Work Experience program is a simulation of actual job. In this program, we allow you to join our team of developers, system administrators, quality analysts, and project manager. You are assigned tecnical tasks just as if you work for us. That is how you gain real work experience. The program is 100% online and our team meetings are Mondays - Fridays, 20 minutes meeting each day.

Why Do I Need it?

Job interview questions are based on real life experience. Not tutorials. Here is were you will learn real life work.

Choose a career path to build your experience from our Areas of Specialization

Accept our “Terms of Service”, make payment and sign up

Complete the Work Experience application, I-9 employment eligibility form, upload copy of paystub to determine eligibility

Finally, read our team meeting rules, agree to follow our rules, join our team and start building your work experience
