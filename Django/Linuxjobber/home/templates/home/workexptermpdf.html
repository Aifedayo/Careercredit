This Student Enrollment Agreement (this “Agreement”), is a binding agreement between you, the Student (the “Student”), and Linuxjobber, Inc. with regard to a single enrollment in an Advanced Career Training program that may consist of a single or multiple programs. This Agreement becomes binding and effective upon the Student’s acceptance, indicated by online acknowledgement of receipt of, and agreement to, the provisions of this Agreement.

The parties here to agree as follows:

ADMISSIONS CRITERIA

The Student has completed technical training in the area of interest and is seeking “hands-on” field experience to better understand the daily responsibilities of a working professional in that area.

TUITION

The Student agrees to pay a one-time, non-refundable registration payment of $399 and additional 14% of the Student’s one-year salary when the Student gains employment in any field related to the area of interest.

COMPLETION REQUIREMENTS

In order to receive a Certificate of Completion, the Student must successfully complete the program by the date indicated on the Stduent’s Linuxjobber profile following the date on which the Student enrolls in the program, and tuition must be paid in full before the Student can receive a Certificate of Completion.

SCHEDULE

The duration of the program is 3 months and the Student is required to attend daily online meetings (Mondays through Friday) for the entire duration of the program. It is the responsibility of the Student to attend the meeting and to meet privately with instructors to cover materials for missed classes.

PROGRAM CANCELLATION POLICY

The Student may request the Student's enrollment be canceled at any time before the Student is registered for and granted access to the program.

REFUND POLICY

For refunds requested within ten (7) calendar days commencement of the Work Experience training, as shown on Student’s Linuxjobber profile, one hundred percent (100%) of the tuition amount shall be For refund requests within Eight (8) to twenty-four (24) calendar days of commencement of the Work Experience training, as shown on Student’s Linuxjobber profile, fifty percent (50%) of the tuition amount shall be refunded.

TRANSFER POLICY

The Student may transfer from one program to another within sixty (60) days following the Student’s registration date. There is a transfer fee of one hundred twenty-five dollars ($125), plus any applicable taxes, if the transfer takes place after the first ten (10) calendar days of the Start Date listed in the Student’s Linuxjobber profile. If the program to which the Student wishes to transfer costs less than the original program in which the Student is registered, the Student shall be credited the difference, minus the transfer fee. If the program to which the Student wishes to transfer costs more than the original program in which the Student is registered, the Student shall be charged the difference, plus the transfer fee and any applicable taxes.

HOLD POLICY

A “hold” is a permitted suspension of the Student’s participation in the program without penalty. The Student may request a hold of up to thirty (30) days and holds will only be granted for those with extenuating circumstances, at Linuxjobber’s discretion, such as military deployment or documented medical concerns. Some program holds may incur a fee and the student shall be made aware of this before the hold is initiated. If the Student has used a loan, they are still responsible for the regular loan payments throughout the hold period. If the Student remains on hold for more than thirty (30) days, the Student shall be dropped from the program without a refund and the Student’s grade shall be shown as Incomplete on the school’s records.
